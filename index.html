CAPT. BIPIN CHANDRA HAZARIKA

Educational Qualification : B.Sc. B.E. (Elect) MIE (India)

After being graduated in Electrical Engineer in 1965 from Assam Engineering College under Gauhati University, I joined Assam State Electricity Board as Asst. Engineer in the office of the Chief Electrical Engineer in Shillong. After nine (9) months of service in ASEB, I got an opportunity to join Indian Army in Short Service Commission. In fact, I was commissioned as Lieutenant on 1st Jan. 1967 after 4 months of Infantry Training in Officers Training School, Madras, after which I was detailed to undergo a technical training in EME School (North) Baroda. After completion of training in EME School (N), Baroda, I was posted to a EME Battalion under 8th Mountain Division in Nagaland. There I was serving in various capacities in the insurgency affected operational area of Nagaland and Manipur. Though I was working as Major in the latest period of my Army Service. I was released as Capt. from the Army after 4 years of commission service including training. After being released from Army, I joined my parent department Assam State Electricity Board and served in this esteemed organisation for the rest of my career in various fields like generation, Transmission, distribution of Electricity including material management, man-management and other techno-administrative field under the jurisdiction of Assam State Electricity Board which includes both hills and plains. Lastly, I was superannuated as Addl. Chief Engineer of Upper Assam Zone from Assam State Electricity Board in February, 1998.
